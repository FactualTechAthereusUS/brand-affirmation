## 2. Protected Health Information and HIPAA

Certain information you provide in connection with healthcare services, including your medical history, diagnoses, prescriptions, and treatment information, constitutes Protected Health Information ("PHI") under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 ("HIPAA").

This Privacy Policy is separate from and supplemental to the Notice of Privacy Practices provided by the Medical Groups and Providers through whose portals your clinical care is administered. We encourage you to review those notices carefully.

Blissley acts as a Business Associate under HIPAA with respect to PHI processed on behalf of covered healthcare entities. We have entered into Business Associate Agreements with all applicable Medical Groups, Providers, and Pharmacies. Our business associates are required to maintain equivalent confidentiality and security standards.

Your PHI will be used and disclosed only as permitted or required by law, including for:

- Treatment, payment, and healthcare operations
- Coordinating care between Providers, Medical Groups, and Pharmacies
- Complying with legal obligations
- Improving the quality and safety of our services

We will never sell your PHI. We will not use or disclose your PHI for marketing purposes without your explicit written authorization.

## 7. California Residents — CCPA/CPRA Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

**Right to Know:** You may request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the sources of that information, the purposes for which it was used, and the categories of third parties with whom it was shared.

**Right to Delete:** You may request deletion of personal information we have collected about you, subject to certain exceptions (including our legal obligations and the need to complete pending transactions).

**Right to Opt Out of Sale or Sharing:** We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising purposes without your consent.

**Right to Correct:** You may request correction of inaccurate personal information we hold about you.

**Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information:** You may request that we limit the use of sensitive personal information to what is necessary to perform the Service.

**Right to Non-Discrimination:** We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights.

In the past 12 months, we have collected the following categories of personal information: identifiers, protected classification characteristics, health and medical information, commercial information, internet activity information, geolocation data, and inferences drawn from the above.

To submit a CCPA request, email support@blissley.com or write to us at our address below. We will verify your identity before processing your request. You may designate an authorized agent to act on your behalf, subject to proof of authorization.
